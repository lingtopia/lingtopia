Development Timeline

Tracking the evolution of Lingua Nova from concept to reality.

January 2021

Project Inception

Initial concept development and research into linguistic frameworks. Formation of the core research team and establishment of project goals.

Milestone: Project Charter
March 2021

Phonological Framework

Development of the sound system, focusing on cross-cultural accessibility and ease of pronunciation. Established 28 core phonemes.

Milestone: Phoneme Inventory
June 2021

Morphological Structure

Creation of word-building rules and grammatical markers. Established agglutinative approach with regular affixation patterns.

Milestone: Morphology Guide
September 2021

Syntax Development

Formulation of sentence structure rules and word order conventions. Adopted a flexible SVO (Subject-Verb-Object) framework.

Milestone: Syntax Rulebook
December 2021

Core Vocabulary

Development of the first 500 essential words, focusing on universal concepts and everyday communication needs.

Milestone: First Dictionary
February 2022

First Academic Paper

Publication of "Lingua Nova: A Novel Approach to Constructed Languages" in the Journal of Linguistic Innovation.

Milestone: Academic Recognition
May 2022

Community Launch

Public introduction of the project and establishment of online community platforms for contributors and enthusiasts.

Milestone: 50 Active Contributors
August 2022

Educational Materials

Development of learning resources, including the first textbook and online course materials for beginners.

Milestone: Learning Platform
November 2022

Expanded Vocabulary

Growth to 1,000+ words, including specialized terminology for academic, scientific, and cultural domains.

Milestone: Comprehensive Dictionary
January 2023

First Translation Project

Collaborative translation of selected literary works into Lingua Nova, testing the language's expressive capabilities.

Milestone: Literary Translation
April 2023

Digital Tools

Launch of translation apps, keyboard layouts, and other digital resources to facilitate language use.

Milestone: Technology Integration
Present

Ongoing Development

Continuous refinement of the language based on user feedback, research findings, and practical applications.

Current Focus: Semantic Expansion
