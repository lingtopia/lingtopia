Contributors

Meet the dedicated individuals behind the Lingua Nova project.

Dr. Elena Moreno

Project Lead & Linguistic Director

Professor of Linguistics with expertise in constructed languages and cross-cultural communication. Founded the Lingua Nova project in 2021.

Website Twitter Publications

Dr. Hiroshi Tanaka

Phonology & Morphology Lead

Computational linguist specializing in phonological systems and language acquisition. Designed the sound system and word-formation rules.

Website Twitter Publications

Prof. Amara Okafor

Syntax & Semantics Lead

Cognitive linguist with research focus on semantic structures and cross-linguistic syntax. Developed the grammatical framework of Lingua Nova.

Website Twitter Publications

Dr. Miguel Santos

Lexicography Director

Lexicographer and etymologist who oversees vocabulary development and maintains the dictionary database. Expert in Romance and Germanic languages.

Website Twitter Publications

Dr. Sarah Chen

Educational Director

Applied linguist specializing in language pedagogy. Develops learning materials and teaching methodologies for Lingua Nova.

Website Twitter Publications

Alex Novak

Technology Lead
